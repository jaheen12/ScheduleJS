Weekly Routine (Simple and Effective)

Workout days: 6 days
Rest: 1 days

Saturday — Chest + Core

Push-ups — 4 × 12

Decline push-ups (feet elevated) — 3 × 10

Diamond push-ups — 3 × 8

Plank — 3 × 45 sec

Leg raises — 3 × 12


Purpose: build chest while tightening stomach.


---

Sunday — Legs

Squats — 4 × 20

Lunges — 3 × 12 each leg

Bulgarian split squats — 3 × 10 each leg

Wall sit — 3 × 40 sec


Strong legs improve hormone response and overall muscle gain.


---

Monday — Shoulders + Core

Pike push-ups — 4 × 10

Shoulder taps — 4 × 20

Plank — 3 × 60 sec

Bicycle crunch — 3 × 20


This widens shoulders and tightens waist.


---

Tuesday — Rest / Light Activity

Do posture work:

Dead hang — 3 × 30 sec

Cobra stretch — 30 sec × 3

Cat–cow stretch — 1 min


Optional: 20-minute walk.


---

Wednesday — Back + Core

If you have a bar:

Pull-ups — 5 × max


If no bar:

Inverted rows under table — 4 × 10

Superman hold — 4 × 30 sec

Reverse snow angels — 3 × 12

Leg raises — 3 × 12


Back training is key for the V-shape.


---

Thursday — Full Body

Push-ups — 3 × 15

Squats — 3 × 20

Pike push-ups — 3 × 10

Plank — 3 × 45 sec

Jump squats — 3 × 12


This day boosts metabolism and overall conditioning.


---

Friday — Rest

Light stretching only.
